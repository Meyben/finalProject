The app
Not only is it easy to look up the receipt when you need to, but you will find all the user manuals and other relevant info when you click the product. You can now organize everything you need to know about your stuff, at the same time as you are saving the environment.

How it works
Our database is open for all retailers to register info and user manuals for their products. To further motivate for a easier life and cleaner environment we offer a kickback deal to the retailers who register products in our database. Each item in the store will be identified thru the bar code via our integrated bar code reader.


Easy to use
The app will save you a lot of free space. Our average consumer stores approximately 1kg of receipts printed on paper each year. Now you get the opportunity to have all possible information just three clicks away.

Save time. Save money. Save the environment.
