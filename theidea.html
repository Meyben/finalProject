Receipts


With todays commercial model every time you buy a product you will get a receipt written on paper. Not only is it hard to keep track of the small pieces of paper, but it also has a huge environmental effect. The US prints receipts worth 640.000 tons of paper each year, or equivalent to 9.6 million trees being cut down. Of course you could just say no thank you to the receipt, but at the same time you will resign all your warranty rights. Surely, there must be a better way?

The idea


We want to give you an opportunity to digitalize all your receipts, directly on your smartphone. Our app will connect to your mobile pay app of choice, and when you make a purchase in the app your items will be registered with info sourced from our database.
